Delicious Pumkin Dip

A perfect dish for fall and sharing with friends. This yummy vanilla pudding dip goes great with graham crackers or vanilla wafers!

Ingredients

  • 16 oz frozen whipped topping, thawed
  • 5 oz instant vanilla pudding mix
  • 15 oz can solid pack pumpkin
  • 1 tsp pumpkin pie spice

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, mix together instant vanilla pudding mix, pumpkin and pumpkin pie spice.
  2. Fold in the thawed frozen whipped topping.
  3. Chill in the refrigerator until serving.
